Hanzi Graph

  • To begin, search for a hanzi. A graph will appear to the right. You can then click on hanzi or the lines that connect them. Example sentences will then appear here!

    Circles (nodes) correspond to individual hanzi characters.

    Connecting lines (edges) represent a relationship among hanzi. Namely, by connecting the two linked hanzi, a word can be formed (or partially formed in the case of 3+ character words).

    Note that you can zoom, drag, and re-arrange the diagram however you please.

    Example sentences and pinyin transcriptions were pulled from Tatoeba, which releases data under CC-BY 2.0 FR.

The color of a given hanzi or connection indicates the level at which it first appears in the HSK exam.
